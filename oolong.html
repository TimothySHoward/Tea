Oolong

Oolong Tea is a semi-Oxidized tea, meaning the leaf has been oxidized anywhere from 15-85%, giving you a very wide variety of flavours within the oolong category.Oolong leaves are picked when they are a little bit larger than other teas. they are withered under the sun and then hand rolled to bruise them and speed up the oxidation process.Once the desired level of oxidation has been reached the leafes then undergo many different processes including pan frying and rolling into either strips or balls
